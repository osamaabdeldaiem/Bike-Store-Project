# 🚴 Bike Store Sales & Inventory Dashboard

An end-to-end **Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard** developed to analyze sales, customers, products, inventory, stores, and employee performance for a bike retail business.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the complete Business Intelligence workflow using the Bike Store dataset. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and KPIs to help analyze sales performance, customer behavior, inventory levels, store performance, and employee productivity.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

- 🏠 Home
- 📈 Sales Overview

- 👥 Customer Analysis
- 🏪 Store Performance
- 👨‍💼 Staff Performance

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value
- Total Customers
- Total Products
- Revenue per Store
- Revenue per Staff
- Best Selling Product
- Best Selling Category
- Low Stock Products
- Out of Stock Products

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Identify top-selling products and categories.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Compare store performance.
- Evaluate employee performance.
- Track inventory levels and low-stock products.
